The ex cinema Ambasciatori reopens as a bookshop and restaurant

The Ambasciatori, a ex red light cinema in via Orefici, has been restructured by Coop Adriatica, and reopens on three floors as a bookshop of the Coop chain and together as a cafeteria, tavern and food shop under the banner of Eataly.

2018

Librerie.coop Ambasciatori

Libreria.coop Ambasciatori offers a daily program of events and cultural activities: book presentations, with famous national and international writers, but also concerts, activities and workshops for young people and children. The Ambassadors project has succeeded in integrating the overall offer of culture and food on all three floors. The success of this project is confirmed by the great response of the public.
